Introduction

ReagenTree is a web application designed to manage laboratory reagents and stocks and keeps track of the reagent inventory, alerting the users when stocks are running low. ReagenTree is webbrowser and operation system angnostic and mobile-friendly, meaning it can be accessed from any device with a web browser including mobile devices. ReagenTree is powered by Django, JavaScript, and Bootstrap the entire code base is open source and can be found on GitHub.

User Account

Registration

You need to register an account to use ReagenTree. To register an account, click on the register link on the login page and fill in the registration form. The registration form requires you to provide your email address, first name, last name, and password. Follow the instructions on the registration form to complete the registration process. Please note, only OUH email address is accepted.

Login

To login to ReagenTree, click on the login link on the home page and enter your email address and password. If you have forgotten your password, click on the forgot password link to reset your password.

Logout

To logout of ReagenTree, click on the logout link on the home page. You will be logged out of the system and redirected to the login page.
