Latest stocks
This panel displays the 10 most recently added stocks in the system. To view details for an individual stock, click on the stock name. To view all stocks, click on the arrow icon in the top right corner of the panel.
Pending validations
This panel displays all stocks pending validation. Click the stock source name to view the stock details and add validations. To switch to the pending validation-only view, click on the arrow icon in the top right corner of the panel.
Please restock
This panel displays all stocks that are running low and need to be restocked. It shows the remaining stock as well as the threshold for restocking. To view details for the stock source item, click on the source item link. To view details for an individual stock, click on the stock lot number. Once an item has been restocked, click on the trolley icon to acknowledge and disable the restock alert.
Search Box
In the search box, type in any text to search for Stocks, Inhouse reagents, Items, Companies, Locations and Categories. To narrow down the search results, type in more search terms separated by a space.
